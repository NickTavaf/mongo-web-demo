For too long, public spaces have been designed without considering the needs of neurodivergent individuals and those with anxiety. Atmos plans to change that by providing real-time information about sensory environments, helping you plan your routes and choose spaces that feel safe and comfortable for you.

Built for You

Atmos is designed specifically for people who experience the world differently. Whether you're navigating sensory sensitivities, social anxiety, or simply need to know what to expect before you arrive, we're here to help.

Our interface is intentionally calm, predictable, and free from overwhelming stimuli. No sudden animations, no bright flashing colors, no unnecessary complexity. Just the information you need, presented in a way that respects your needs.
